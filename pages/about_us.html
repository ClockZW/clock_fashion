Our Story Crafting Excellence Since Day One

Clock Fashion was born from a passion for quality craftsmanship and a commitment to serving the Harare community with premium shirts that blend style, comfort, and local pride.

Get In Touch View Our Collection

Founded on Quality & Community

Clock Fashion began as a vision to bring premium quality shirts to the heart of Harare. Located in the vibrant Timire Park area, we've built our reputation on exceptional craftsmanship, personalized service, and deep community connections.

Our journey started with a simple belief: every person deserves access to well-made, stylish shirts that reflect their personality and professional aspirations. From formal business attire to casual weekend wear, we've dedicated ourselves to sourcing and creating shirts that meet the diverse needs of Zimbabwe's fashion-conscious individuals.

Quality First

Every shirt in our collection is carefully selected for superior fabric quality, construction, and durability.

Community Focus

We're proud to be a local Harare business, supporting our community and understanding local fashion needs.

Personal Service

We believe in building relationships with our customers, offering personalized styling advice and exceptional service.
